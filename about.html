Exploring the connections between marketing and design.

Hey! I’m Ellie, an aspiring UX/UI Designer with a passion for creating a space where aesthetic meets function.

Growing up, I had developed a love for art and all things creative. I freely explored graphic design, fine art, and digital illustration. However, as time passed and I grew older, I eventually made the distinction that creativity was a hobby and not a career.

It wasn’t until I had endured a miserable year as a biology major, that I realized that I couldn’t pursue a career I wasn’t passionate about. Immediately, I ditched pre-med and dove head first into marketing where I could rediscover my creative potential.

Today, I am fascinated by data analytics and consumer psychology. I spend every day finding the connections between what I learn in class and functional design. I love what I do and I’m looking forward to seeing where this journey leads me!
